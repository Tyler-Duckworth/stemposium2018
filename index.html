What is a computer?

In Hidden Figures, Katherine Johnson, the main character, is a “computer.”

At that time, the term “computer” simply meant someone who computes, or makes calculations.

Nowadays, “computer” simply means an electronic device that stores and processes data.

These devices can range from phones to computers to clocks.

Hidden Figures also included the introduction of the IBM 7090 to NASA. In the movie, the IBM plays a major role in the lives of human "computers."

sThe IBM 7090 was a vacuum tube mainframe computer that started production in 1959. It took punch cards as input, punched in a way to allow for the IBM to create FORTRAN programs from them. It could take up over 100 ft2.

Altogether, the IBM could make up to 24,000 multiplications per second.
